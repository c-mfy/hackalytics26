about speechwrite
speechwrite evaluates ai speech-to-text tools by comparing transcriptions to true spoken content, uncovering accuracy gaps across accents and speaker groups. in entertainment, this matters: subtitles, streaming, gaming voice chat, and global media accessibility are all affected by accent, regional, non-native speaker, dialect, and code-switching bias.
